Risotto Recipe

Learn how to make risotto like a pro and master the simple cooking technique. Continuously stirring and gradually adding the ingredients is key.

Ingredients

  • arborio rice
  • parmesan cheese
  • butter
  • wine

Steps

  1. In a large pot add the vegetable stock and heat over medium-low heat until warmed to about 120 to 130ºF (49 to 54ºC), about 10 minutes.
  2. Heat a separate dutch oven or large saute pan with high sides over medium heat. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil, once hot add the onions and garlic. Cook until soft and translucent, about 3 minutes.
  3. Add the rice, stir and cook until the grains are coated with oil and lightly toasted, about 2 minutes.
